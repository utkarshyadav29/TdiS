About TDiS

Engineering excellence delivered with precision — from concept to completion, for industries that power the world.

Who We Are

Expertise For Creative Design

We provide engineering services for coded and non-coded pressure vessel heat exchangers, distillation columns, reactors, storage tanks, skid mounted assembly and piping systems for petrochemical refineries, fertilizers, chemicals, power generation plants, steel plants, sugar, cement and other allied industries.

From engineering to application development, knowledge management to business process management — we help our clients find the right problems to solve, and to solve these effectively.

Along with our team of incredibly bright veteran engineers and designers, we take your requirement and define it to perfection — delivering a sustainable and best-in-class product.

Our Mission

Engineering Consultancy at its Finest

Tech Design Industrial Services is a leading global player in Mechanical, Civil, Chemical and Renewable Engineering Design and IT Consulting.

TDiS, an India based organization, is in the business of engineering consultancy services — Process Skid Packages, Cladded / Non-Cladded Pressure Vessels, Deaerators & Heat Exchangers, API650 Tanks, and all supporting structures for all types of industries based on country codes and standards.
